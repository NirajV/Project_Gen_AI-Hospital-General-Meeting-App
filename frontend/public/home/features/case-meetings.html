Microsoft Teams, automatic

A Teams meeting is generated through Microsoft Graph at creation time. Change the date and the Teams meeting reschedules itself — same link, new time, every recipient updated.

Calendar invites that just work

Every invite carries a standards-compliant .ics attachment. Outlook, Google, Apple — recipients add the event with one click, in their own time-zone, with the correct recurrence rule.

Time-zone correct

The organiser's time-zone is stored on the meeting; participants always see local time in the email body, the .ics attachment, and the in-app card.

A meeting workspace, not a calendar slot.

Recurrence the way clinicians schedule

Daily, weekly, fortnightly, monthly, quarterly or yearly — every recurrence rule is RFC-5545 compliant, so the .ics attachment expands correctly in every calendar app you put it in front of.

Holiday-aware

Default holiday packs for US, UK and India are built in; your hospital's custom days layer on top. The wizard refuses to create a meeting on a blocked date — no more accidental Republic Day tumour boards.

Automatic reminders

A single, deduplicated reminder email goes to each accepted participant one hour before the meeting. The scheduler is configurable, idempotent, and resilient to restarts.

Auto-completion

Meetings flip to completed ten minutes after their scheduled end. Decisions and treatment plans entered during the call are preserved; the dashboard never carries stale "in progress" items.
