Versioned treatment plans

Audit trail by default

Every change captures who, what and when. Read-only export for clinical governance review and inclusion in the medical record.

Why a seven-day edit window matters.

Most meeting platforms either lock the record at completion (frustrating clinicians who think of one more clarifying note on the drive home) or leave it open forever (frustrating clinical governance and the medical record). Seven days strikes the balance — long enough for legitimate post-meeting refinement, short enough to make the record final.

  • Each edit appends a versioned entry; nothing is overwritten silently.
  • The clock starts when the meeting flips to completed.
  • After day seven, the plan is read-only — but the discussion thread on the case can continue.
