Feature · Patient management

The patient is on the agenda — not in an attachment.

Every patient is a first-class record in BioMedMeet, with their own profile, file vault, decision history, and a built-in approval workflow when non-organisers add them to a meeting.

Request a demo

Fourteen clinical fields

Identity, MRN, gender, date of birth, contact, address, department, provider, diagnosis, allergies, medications, notes — captured once, available everywhere the patient appears.

Files attached to the case

Radiology, pathology, lab, consult notes — typed and tagged at upload. Files attach to both the patient record and the meeting agenda for that patient.

Organiser approval workflow

Non-organisers can propose adding a patient. The organiser sees a tick / cross approval button on the patient card before the patient enters the agenda.

Built for how clinicians actually search.

Search by MRN, name, or diagnosis

The patient list supports prefix and substring search across name, MRN and primary diagnosis, with department and provider filters.

History at a glance

Every meeting the patient has appeared in — with the agenda question, the decision logged, and the treatment plan recorded — is visible from the patient profile.

Exportable for the medical record

Server-side PDF export captures the full case discussion, decisions, and treatment plan — ready to attach to your hospital's record system.

Privacy-respecting access controls

Only meeting participants and patient approvers can see the case. Role gates surface in the API as well as the UI.
