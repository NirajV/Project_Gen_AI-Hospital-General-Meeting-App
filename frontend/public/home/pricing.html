FAQ

Common questions.

What does the $25,000 cover?

A perpetual licence to run the current version of BioMedMeet on your infrastructure, for unlimited users, plus the two-week installation and onboarding engagement and the two-month warranty trial.

What happens if we don't like it during the trial?

You uninstall the containers and walk away. We bill nothing. We don't ask why, but if you tell us we'll roll your feedback into the next release.

Are software updates included?

Patch and minor updates for twelve months from go-live are included. After year one, a small annual maintenance fee (negotiated separately) keeps you on the latest stable release. You can also choose to freeze your install — it'll keep running.

Do you need access to our patient data?

No. BioMedMeet runs entirely inside your network. We never see patient identifiers, diagnoses, or meeting recordings. Support sessions happen on screen-shares your IT team initiates.

Can we host multiple hospitals on one install?

The standard licence covers one hospital / clinical entity. For a hospital group, ask us about multi-tenant pricing — we keep it well under per-site list.

Ready to see it in your meeting room?

Fifteen minutes is enough to know if BioMedMeet fits how your case meetings actually run. Book a demo or write to us directly — we'll respond same-day during US business hours.
